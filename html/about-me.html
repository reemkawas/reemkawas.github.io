About Me

Driven by a strong interest in both technology and creativity, I enjoy building solutions that are not only functional but also visually appealing. My background has allowed me to develop a solid understanding of computer systems, including hardware, software, and logical problem-solving, along with experience creating system diagrams and flowcharts to clearly map out ideas and processes. To ensure projects are planned and easily understood, I have experience in creating structured documentation, including feasibility studies, scope documents, and user manuals.

I am continuously expanding my expertise by pursuing certifications through AI coursework, the Google Cybersecurity Certificate, and the Microsoft Power BI Certificate, with the goal of specializing as a Cloud Architect. I’m especially interested in how systems can be designed to be efficient, adaptable, and impactful in modern world applications.

Aside from my technical skills, I have a strong creative side as a graphic designer, where I’ve worked on websites, logos, and advertisements that balance both technicality and visual appeal. I also enjoy expressing creativity through music, drawing, and photo and video editing, which adds a unique touch to everything I create.

In business, I excel as a Sales Lead at Pandora, where I manage daily operations, make decisions, and lead with strong communication and leadership skills. LEARNING AND DESIGN
